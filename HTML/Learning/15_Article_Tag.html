About College

Indraprastha Institute of Technology and Management (IITM) is one of the well-known institutes in Delhi, affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). The college offers undergraduate courses in fields like B.Tech, BCA, BBA, and BJMC, focusing on academic excellence and overall development.
IITM is known for its experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and a student-friendly environment. It encourages students to participate in various technical, cultural, and sports activities. The college also hosts seminars, workshops, and placement drives to prepare students for the industry.
With its commitment to quality education and skill development, IITM continues to shape future professionals and responsible citizens.
